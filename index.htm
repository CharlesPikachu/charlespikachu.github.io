Zhenchao Jin

I am currently a PhD student at MedAI Lab in The University of Hong Kong (HKU), supervised by Prof. Lequan Yu. Before that, I received my master degree at Visual Intelligence and Security (VIS) research group in University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) led by Prof. Nenghai Yu.


My research interests focus on machine learning and computer vision, specifically on the topic of semantic segmentation and object detection. Now, I'm also interested in domain adaptation, continual learning and casual inference.
